About the Project

Our Plant Disease prediction system uses advanced deep learning to identify plant leaf diseases from images. By analyzing patterns, colors, and textures, it helps farmers and gardeners make informed decisions. Early detection ensures healthier crops, reduces pesticide misuse, and increases productivity. The model is trained on thousands of plant images to deliver accurate predictions across multiple species. Our mission is to simplify agriculture diagnostics through accessible AI technology. This web app provides an intuitive interface to upload images and get instant disease analysis. It empowers users with real-time feedback and confidence levels.
